Code: P0420 - Catalyst System Efficiency Below Threshold
Location: Bank 1 (Cylinder side with #1 cylinder)

WHAT THIS MEANS:
- Catalytic converter not cleaning exhaust efficiently
- Downstream O2 sensor detecting insufficient conversion
- May cause emissions test failure
- Usually not drivability issue initially
